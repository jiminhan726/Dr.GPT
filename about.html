What is Dr. GPT?

Dr. GPT is an AI-powered assistant designed to offer friendly, non-diagnostic medical guidance based on your symptoms. It uses advanced language models to give you a sense of direction — always reminding you to consult a real doctor.

Our Mission

We believe in accessible, empathetic health guidance for everyone. Our goal is to empower people with AI tools that feel like a conversation with a caring professional — even when you're just looking for a starting point.

Meet the Creator

This project was built by a passionate student developer who dreams of becoming a pediatrician. Inspired by a love for healthcare and technology, Dr. GPT was created as a free, educational tool for curious minds.
